About Rhonda

I have a deep respect for and special gift with animals — cats, dogs, rabbits, birds, iguanas, and bearded dragons.

I have worked 5 years in veterinary nursing, volunteered 3 years at the San Francisco Zoo, before starting my own business, so I am able to provide extra love and attention to special needs animals.

Thank you for trusting me with the care of your animal friend(s).

  • Pictures and text updates so you know your pet is happy and safe.

Reiki and energy healing for people or animals ($2/min):

Different people have different beliefs, but results speak for themselves. If you or your animal are having health challenges, try an energy healing.
